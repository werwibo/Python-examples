xbox must be connected via usb to your computer.

With round left joystick you can move the green rectangle (gun) to left or right.
When you press button a (blue cross), then a shoot in top-direction starts.


From the top, red squares are falling.
If the bullet has collision with a red square, than hits are increased.
Each shooting increases the shoots.

You hav 30 seconds to play.

The rest seconds are presented.

Score in the end is the better, the more hits and the less missing shoots.

If e.g. you have 10 hits with 20 shoots, score = 10 * (10 / 20) = 5
